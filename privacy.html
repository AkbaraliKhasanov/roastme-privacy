Privacy Policy

Effective: May 31, 2026

Short version: We don't collect personal data, don't create accounts, and don't track you. Your selfies and bio text are sent to OpenAI only to generate a roast, then discarded. Your roast history stays on your device.

1. Who we are

"AI Roast Me" (the "App") is operated by an independent developer ("we", "us", "our"). This Privacy Policy explains what data the App collects, how we use it, and the choices you have.

2. Information we collect

2.1 Information you provide

  • Selfie photos — when you use Selfie Roast, the image is sent to our backend and then to OpenAI for analysis. The image is not stored on our servers. OpenAI processes it under their own policy.
  • Bio text — when you use Bio Roast or Party Mode, the text you type is sent to our backend and then to OpenAI for analysis. It is not stored on our servers.

2.2 Information collected automatically

  • Anonymous device identifier — a random UUID generated on your device, used only for rate-limiting (3 free roasts per day). This identifier cannot be linked back to you and is not tied to your Apple ID, email, name, or any other personal information.
  • Standard server logs — IP address, request timestamp, and User-Agent. These are kept for up to 14 days for security and abuse-prevention purposes only.

2.3 Information stored on your device

  • Roast history — your past roasts (text, emoji rating, date) are stored locally in the App via UserDefaults. They never leave your device and we cannot access them. You can clear this history at any time.
  • Settings — onboarding state, daily roast count.

3. What we do NOT collect

  • We do not create user accounts.
  • We do not ask for your name, email, phone number, or social profiles.
  • We do not use analytics SDKs, advertising IDs, or fingerprinting.
  • We do not share data with advertisers.
  • We do not track you across apps or websites.

4. How we use information

The data we receive is used only to:

  • Generate the roast you requested.
  • Enforce the daily free roast limit.
  • Protect the service from abuse and bots.
  • Maintain and improve the App.

5. Third-party services

We use a small number of third parties strictly necessary to operate the App:

  • OpenAI — generates the roast text. Your selfie or bio is sent to OpenAI's API. See OpenAI's Privacy Policy.
  • Apple StoreKit — handles in-app purchases and subscriptions. Purchase data is handled by Apple under Apple's Privacy Policy.
  • Hosting provider — our backend runs on a server provided by Hetzner Online GmbH (Germany).

6. Data retention

  • Selfie photos and bio text — not stored after the roast is generated.
  • Server logs — up to 14 days.
  • On-device history — until you delete it or uninstall the App.

7. Children's privacy

The App is rated 17+ and is not intended for users under 17. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has used the App, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

8. Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Delete all on-device history by deleting the App.
  • Request information about any data we may have related to your anonymous device identifier (within the 14-day server log window) by contacting us.
  • If you are in the EU/EEA, UK, or California: rights under GDPR / UK GDPR / CCPA, including access, deletion, and objection.

9. Security

All communication between the App and our backend uses HTTPS (TLS). API keys are stored as server environment variables and never embedded in the App.

10. International transfers

Our backend is hosted in Germany. Roast requests are sent to OpenAI, which may process data in the United States. By using the App you consent to these transfers.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Effective" date above shows when it was last changed. Material changes will be announced in-App or via the App Store update notes.
